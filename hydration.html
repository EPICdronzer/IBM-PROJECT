HYDRATION CALCULATOR

Proper hydration is essential for maintaining good health and overall well-being. Use this calculator to estimate your daily water intake based on various factors.

General recommendations for daily water intake:

  • 2.7 liters (91 ounces) for women
  • 3.7 liters (125 ounces) for men

Staying hydrated is crucial for maintaining the balance of bodily fluids, which are essential for processes like digestion, absorption, circulation, and temperature regulation. Adequate hydration helps keep your skin healthy, aids in the transport of nutrients, and improves cognitive function. Proper water intake also supports joint lubrication and reduces the risk of kidney stones. Factors such as exercise, environment, and overall health can impact your hydration needs. Make sure to drink more water in hot climates and during physical activity to replace fluids lost through sweat and maintain optimal bodily functions.
